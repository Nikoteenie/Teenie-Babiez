Nikoteenie's Teenie Babiez are a projection of her own Sub-Personalities. She categorizies their contrasting behaviors through colors and aesthetics. In doing so she has found that she is able to better understand herself and the choices that she makes. Moxies, Bias and Sense are what she calls, her "Main Personalities", that she find to be most significant and most reflected upon. Nikoteenie hopes in sharing her research of self-understanding the viewer will have the urge to do the same.
